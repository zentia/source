详细描述

Finite state machine.

/-----------—\ / V 0 --> 1--> ptr --> -1 ^ | | V ptr|1

"owner" = corresponding server_thread. Odd states (except -1) indicate that someone is executing code on the job. Most transitions driven only by owner. Transition 0-->-1 is driven by non-owner. Transition ptr->-1 is driven by owner or non-owner.
