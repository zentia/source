详细描述

A View to display a Spine skeleton. The skeleton can be loaded from an asset bundle (SpineView#loadFromAssets(String, String, Context, SpineController)), local files (SpineView#loadFromFile(File, File, Context, SpineController)), URLs (SpineView#loadFromHttp(URL, URL, File, Context, SpineController)), or a pre-loaded AndroidSkeletonDrawable using (SpineView#loadFromDrawable(AndroidSkeletonDrawable, Context, SpineController)).

The skeleton displayed by a SpineView can be controlled via a SpineController.

The size of the widget can be derived from the bounds provided by a BoundsProvider. If the widget is not sized by the bounds computed by the BoundsProvider, the widget will use the computed bounds to fit the skeleton inside the widget's dimensions.
