详细描述

A batch that renders polygons and performs tinting using a light and dark color.

Because an additional vertex attribute is used, the Batch and PolygonBatch methods that accept float[] vertex data do not perform two color tinting. drawTwoColor(Texture, float[], int, int) and drawTwoColor(Texture, float[], int, int, short[], int, int) are provided to accept float[] vertex data that contains two colors per vertex.
