详细描述

A AndroidSkeletonDrawable bundles loading updating updating an AndroidTextureAtlas, Skeleton, and AnimationState into a single easy-to-use class.

Use the AndroidSkeletonDrawable#fromAsset(String, String, Context), AndroidSkeletonDrawable#fromFile(File, File), or AndroidSkeletonDrawable#fromHttp(URL, URL, File) methods to construct a AndroidSkeletonDrawable. To have multiple skeleton drawable instances share the same AndroidTextureAtlas and SkeletonData, use the constructor.

You can then directly access the AndroidSkeletonDrawable#getAtlas(), AndroidSkeletonDrawable#getSkeletonData(), AndroidSkeletonDrawable#getSkeleton(), AndroidSkeletonDrawable#getAnimationStateData(), and AndroidSkeletonDrawable#getAnimationState() to query and animate the skeleton. Use the AnimationState to queue animations on one or more tracks via AnimationState#setAnimation(int, Animation, boolean) or AnimationState#addAnimation(int, Animation, boolean, float).

To update the AnimationState and apply it to the Skeleton, call the AndroidSkeletonDrawable#update(float) function, providing it a delta time in seconds to advance the animations.

To render the current pose of the Skeleton, use SkeletonRenderer#render(Skeleton), SkeletonRenderer#renderToCanvas(Canvas, Array), SkeletonRenderer#renderToBitmap(float, float, int, Skeleton), depending on your needs.
