Random level generator 

The random level generator is an important part of skip-\/list algorithm. The node height in the skip-\/list have a probabilistic distribution where half of the nodes that have level {\ttfamily i} pointers also have level {\ttfamily i+1} pointers (i = 0..30). So, the height of a node is in range \mbox{[}0..31\mbox{]}.
