\doxylink{bench__gemm_8cpp_addc86e8508f14411ec98f521c520f875}{A} pipe represents a stage of a pipeline. \doxylink{bench__gemm_8cpp_addc86e8508f14411ec98f521c520f875}{A} pipe can be either {\itshape parallel} direction or {\itshape serial} direction (specified by \doxylink{namespacetf_abb7a11e41fd457f69e7ff45d4c769564}{tf\+::\+Pipe\+Type}) and is coupled with a callable to invoke by the pipeline scheduler. The callable must take a referenced \doxylink{classtf_1_1_pipeflow}{tf\+::\+Pipeflow} object in the first argument\+:

The pipeflow object is used to query the statistics of a scheduling token in the pipeline, such as pipe, line, and token numbers.
