\doxylink{classeastl_1_1insert__iterator}{insert\+\_\+iterator}

We borrow a trick from SGI STL here and define an \doxylink{classeastl_1_1insert__iterator}{insert\+\_\+iterator} specialization for slist. This allows slist insertions to be O(1) instead of O(n/2), due to caching of the previous node.
