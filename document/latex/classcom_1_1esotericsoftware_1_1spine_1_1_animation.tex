Applies the animation\textquotesingle{}s timelines to the specified skeleton. 

See \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1_animation_1_1_timeline}{Timeline} {\ttfamily \doxylink{}{Timeline\#apply(\+Skeleton, float, float, Array, float, Mix\+Blend, Mix\+Direction)}}. 
\begin{DoxyParams}{参数}
{\em skeleton} & The skeleton the animation is being applied to. This provides access to the bones, slots, and other skeleton components the timelines may change. \\
\hline
{\em last\+Time} & The last time in seconds this animation was applied. Some timelines trigger only at specific times rather than every frame. Pass -\/1 the first time an animation is applied to ensure frame 0 is triggered. \\
\hline
{\em time} & The time in seconds the skeleton is being posed for. Most timelines find the frame before and the frame after this time and interpolate between the frame values. If beyond the {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1_animation_aacf1c53c61a36e8a3f75683a5d562e57}{get\+Duration()}} and {\ttfamily loop} is true then the animation will repeat, else the last frame will be applied. \\
\hline
{\em loop} & If true, the animation repeats after the {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1_animation_aacf1c53c61a36e8a3f75683a5d562e57}{get\+Duration()}}. \\
\hline
{\em events} & If any events are fired, they are added to this list. Can be null to ignore fired events or if no timelines fire events. \\
\hline
{\em alpha} & 0 applies the current or setup values (depending on {\ttfamily blend}). 1 applies the timeline values. Between 0 and 1 applies values between the current or setup values and the timeline values. By adjusting {\ttfamily alpha} over time, an animation can be mixed in or out. {\ttfamily alpha} can also be useful to apply animations on top of each other (layering). \\
\hline
{\em blend} & Controls how mixing is applied when {\ttfamily alpha} \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<} 1. \\
\hline
{\em direction} & Indicates whether the timelines are mixing in or out. Used by timelines which perform instant transitions, such as {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1_animation_1_1_draw_order_timeline}{Draw\+Order\+Timeline}} or {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1_animation_1_1_attachment_timeline}{Attachment\+Timeline}}. \\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
