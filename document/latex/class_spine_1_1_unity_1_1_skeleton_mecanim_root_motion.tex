Add this component to a \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_skeleton_mecanim}{Skeleton\+Mecanim} Game\+Object to turn motion of a selected root bone into Transform or Rigid\+Body motion. Local bone translation movement is used as motion. All top-\/level bones of the skeleton are moved to compensate the root motion bone location, keeping the distance relationship between bones intact.

\doxysubsection{详细描述}
Add this component to a \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_skeleton_mecanim}{Skeleton\+Mecanim} Game\+Object to turn motion of a selected root bone into Transform or Rigid\+Body motion. Local bone translation movement is used as motion. All top-\/level bones of the skeleton are moved to compensate the root motion bone location, keeping the distance relationship between bones intact. 

Only compatible with {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_skeleton_mecanim}{Skeleton\+Mecanim}}. For {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_skeleton_animation}{Skeleton\+Animation}} or {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_skeleton_graphic}{Skeleton\+Graphic}} please use \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_skeleton_root_motion}{Skeleton\+Root\+Motion} instead.
