\doxysubsection{详细描述}
Lock-\/free free list based on tagged pointers (required double-\/width CAS) 

This variant of {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcds_1_1intrusive_1_1_free_list}{Free\+List}} is intended for processor architectures that support double-\/width CAS. It uses \href{https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tagged_pointer}{\texttt{ tagged pointer}} technique to solve ABA problem.
