Bit reversal is a significant part of split-\/list. There are several predefined algorithm in {\ttfamily \doxylink{namespacecds_1_1algo_1_1bit__reversal}{cds\+::algo\+::bit\+\_\+reversal}} namespace, {\ttfamily \doxylink{structcds_1_1algo_1_1bit__reversal_1_1lookup}{cds\+::algo\+::bit\+\_\+reversal\+::lookup}} is the best general purpose one.
