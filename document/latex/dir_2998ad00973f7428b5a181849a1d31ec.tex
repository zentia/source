\doxysubsection{详细描述}
Dear \doxylink{namespace_im_gui}{Im\+Gui} outputs 16-\/bit vertex indices by default. Allegro doesn\textquotesingle{}t support them natively, so we have two solutions\+: convert the indices manually in \doxylink{imgui__impl__allegro5_8cpp}{imgui\+\_\+impl\+\_\+allegro5.\+cpp}, or compile dear imgui with 32-\/bit indices. You can either modify \doxylink{imconfig_8h}{imconfig.\+h} that comes with Dear \doxylink{namespace_im_gui}{Im\+Gui} (easier), or set a C++ preprocessor option IMGUI\+\_\+\+USER\+\_\+\+CONFIG to find to a filename. We are providing {\ttfamily \doxylink{imconfig__allegro5_8h}{imconfig\+\_\+allegro5.\+h}} that enables 32-\/bit indices. Note that the backend supports {\itshape BOTH} 16-\/bit and 32-\/bit indices, but 32-\/bit indices will be slightly faster as they won\textquotesingle{}t require a manual conversion.\hypertarget{README.md_autotoc_md288}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{How to Build}{How to Build}}\label{README.md_autotoc_md288}
