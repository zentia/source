\doxysubsection{详细描述}
Demonstrates how to let the target bone of an IK constraint follow the mouse or touch position, which in turn repositions part of the skeleton, in this case \doxylink{class_spineboy}{Spineboy}\textquotesingle{}s back arm including his gun.
