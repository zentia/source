\doxysubsection{详细描述}
Collects each \doxylink{classspine_1_1_bounding_box_attachment}{Bounding\+Box\+Attachment} that is visible and computes the world vertices for its polygon. The polygon vertices are provided along with convenience methods for doing hit detection.
