class Eigen\+::\+Sparse\+QR$<$ \+\_\+\+Matrix\+Type, \+\_\+\+Ordering\+Type $>$}
\doxylink{struct_eigen_1_1_sparse}{Sparse} left-\/looking rank-\/revealing QR factorization 

This class implements a left-\/looking rank-\/revealing QR decomposition of sparse matrices. When a column has a norm less than a given tolerance it is implicitly permuted to the end. The QR factorization thus obtained is given by A\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}P = Q\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}R where R is upper triangular or trapezoidal.

P is the column permutation which is the product of the fill-\/reducing and the rank-\/revealing permutations. Use \doxylink{class_eigen_1_1_sparse_q_r_a6b1d60d2814988ca581856284d5b8607}{cols\+Permutation()} to get it.

Q is the orthogonal matrix represented as products of Householder reflectors. Use \doxylink{class_eigen_1_1_sparse_q_r_a9ba8e2b8ee3b62c16801ba43bc0c05b8}{matrix\+Q()} to get an expression and \doxylink{class_eigen_1_1_sparse_q_r_a9ba8e2b8ee3b62c16801ba43bc0c05b8}{matrix\+Q()}.\doxylink{adjoint_8cpp_a7333648f53414b05be9e4fd2f009fc22}{adjoint()} to get the adjoint. You can then apply it to a vector.

R is the sparse triangular or trapezoidal matrix. The later occurs when \doxylink{bench__gemm_8cpp_addc86e8508f14411ec98f521c520f875}{A} is rank-\/deficient. \doxylink{class_eigen_1_1_sparse_q_r_aa8e9dddc64fe7382409e29b0f848bf52}{matrix\+R()}.top\+Left\+Corner(\doxylink{ittnotify__static_8h_a4d59258314b5b7d431d257e3a5a98b57}{rank()}, \doxylink{ittnotify__static_8h_a4d59258314b5b7d431d257e3a5a98b57}{rank()}) always returns a triangular factor of full rank.
