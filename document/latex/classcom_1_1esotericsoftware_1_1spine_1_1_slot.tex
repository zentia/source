\doxysubsection{详细描述}
Stores a slot\textquotesingle{}s current pose. Slots organize attachments for {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1_skeleton_a68f7acd27e0071cc78c09cc1e5f00a5e}{Skeleton\#draw\+Order}} purposes and provide a place to store state for an attachment. \doxylink{struct_state}{State} cannot be stored in an attachment itself because attachments are stateless and may be shared across multiple skeletons.
