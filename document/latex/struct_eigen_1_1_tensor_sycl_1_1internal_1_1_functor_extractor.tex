struct \doxylink{struct_eigen_1_1_tensor_sycl_1_1internal_1_1_functor_extractor}{Functor\+Extractor}\+: This struct is used to extract the functors constructed on the host-\/side, to pack them and reuse them in reconstruction of the expression on the device. We have to do that as in \doxylink{namespace_eigen}{Eigen} the functors are not stateless so we cannot re-\/instantiate them on the device. We have to pass instantiated functors to the device.
