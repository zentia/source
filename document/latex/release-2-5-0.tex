\doxysection{Release 2.5.0 (2020/06/01)}
\hypertarget{release-2-5-0}{}\label{release-2-5-0}\index{Release 2.5.0 (2020/06/01)@{Release 2.5.0 (2020/06/01)}}
Starting from v2.\+5.\+0, we have renamed Cpp-\/\+Taskflow to {\itshape Taskflow} to broaden its impact and support. Taskflow will explore multiple scopes of applications and language bindings, rather than just C++. This also made Taskflow naming more succinct and concise.

Taskflow 2.\+5.\+0 is the 7th release in the 2.\+x line! This release includes several new changes such as CPU-\/\+GPU tasking, web-\/based profiler, documentation, and unit tests.\hypertarget{release-2-5-0_release-2-5-0_download}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Download}{Download}}\label{release-2-5-0_release-2-5-0_download}
