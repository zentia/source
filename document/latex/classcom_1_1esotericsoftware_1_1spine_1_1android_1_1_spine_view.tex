\doxysubsection{详细描述}
\doxylink{bench__gemm_8cpp_addc86e8508f14411ec98f521c520f875}{A} {\ttfamily \doxylink{}{View}} to display a \doxylink{namespace_spine}{Spine} skeleton. The skeleton can be loaded from an asset bundle ({\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1android_1_1_spine_view_acc1c47247f9d9ca632388149c3e04172}{Spine\+View\#load\+From\+Assets(\+String, String, Context, Spine\+Controller)}}), local files ({\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1android_1_1_spine_view_ad7d6215547da8a68541999eea4ed372d}{Spine\+View\#load\+From\+File(\+File, File, Context, Spine\+Controller)}}), URLs ({\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1android_1_1_spine_view_ac47516869dbbc6c67ee16fcd2101f836}{Spine\+View\#load\+From\+Http(\+URL, URL, File, Context, Spine\+Controller)}}), or a pre-\/loaded {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1android_1_1_android_skeleton_drawable}{Android\+Skeleton\+Drawable}} using ({\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1android_1_1_spine_view_afbcbb0d96a53610786774a8cce482ac7}{Spine\+View\#load\+From\+Drawable(\+Android\+Skeleton\+Drawable, Context, Spine\+Controller)}}).

The skeleton displayed by a {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1android_1_1_spine_view}{Spine\+View}} can be controlled via a {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1android_1_1_spine_controller}{Spine\+Controller}}.

The size of the widget can be derived from the bounds provided by a {\ttfamily \doxylink{interfacecom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1android_1_1bounds_1_1_bounds_provider}{Bounds\+Provider}}. If the widget is not sized by the bounds computed by the {\ttfamily \doxylink{interfacecom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1android_1_1bounds_1_1_bounds_provider}{Bounds\+Provider}}, the widget will use the computed bounds to fit the skeleton inside the widget\textquotesingle{}s dimensions.
