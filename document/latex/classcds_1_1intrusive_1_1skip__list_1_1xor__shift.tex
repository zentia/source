Xor-\/shift random level generator 

The simplest of the generators described in George Marsaglia\textquotesingle{}s "{}\+Xorshift RNGs"{} paper. This is not a high-\/quality generator but is acceptable for skip-\/list.

The random generator should return numbers from range \mbox{[}0 .. Max\+Height -\/ 1\mbox{]}.

From Doug Lea\textquotesingle{}s Concurrent\+Skip\+List\+Map.\+java.
