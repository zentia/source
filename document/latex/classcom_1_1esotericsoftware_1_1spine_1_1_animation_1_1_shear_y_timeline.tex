Applies this timeline to the skeleton. 
\begin{DoxyParams}{参数}
{\em skeleton} & The skeleton to which the timeline is being applied. This provides access to the bones, slots, and other skeleton components that the timeline may change. \\
\hline
{\em last\+Time} & The last time in seconds this timeline was applied. Timelines such as {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1_animation_1_1_event_timeline}{Event\+Timeline}} trigger only at specific times rather than every frame. In that case, the timeline triggers everything between {\ttfamily last\+Time} (exclusive) and {\ttfamily time} (inclusive). Pass -\/1 the first time an animation is applied to ensure frame 0 is triggered. \\
\hline
{\em time} & The time in seconds that the skeleton is being posed for. Most timelines find the frame before and the frame after this time and interpolate between the frame values. If beyond the last frame, the last frame will be applied. \\
\hline
{\em events} & If any events are fired, they are added to this list. Can be null to ignore fired events or if the timeline does not fire events. \\
\hline
{\em alpha} & 0 applies the current or setup value (depending on {\ttfamily blend}). 1 applies the timeline value. Between 0 and 1 applies a value between the current or setup value and the timeline value. By adjusting {\ttfamily alpha} over time, an animation can be mixed in or out. {\ttfamily alpha} can also be useful to apply animations on top of each other (layering). \\
\hline
{\em blend} & Controls how mixing is applied when {\ttfamily alpha} \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<} 1. \\
\hline
{\em direction} & Indicates whether the timeline is mixing in or out. Used by timelines which perform instant transitions, such as {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1_animation_1_1_draw_order_timeline}{Draw\+Order\+Timeline}} or {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1_animation_1_1_attachment_timeline}{Attachment\+Timeline}}, and others such as {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1esotericsoftware_1_1spine_1_1_animation_1_1_scale_timeline}{Scale\+Timeline}}. \\
