\doxysubsection{详细描述}
The Farm skeleton, with Emitter ({\ttfamily \doxylink{classff_1_1ff__farm_a28b9c788ddcfcb6cdad0f5dfc2b46e80}{lb\+\_\+t}}) and Collector ({\ttfamily \doxylink{classff_1_1ff__farm_a6cfb8f61c59e30639b95479b466a81ba}{gt\+\_\+t}}). 

The Farm skeleton can be seen as a 3-\/stages pipeline. The first stage is the {\itshape Emitter} (\doxylink{classff_1_1ff__loadbalancer}{lb\+\_\+t}) that act as a load-\/balancer; the last (optional) stage would be the {\itshape Collector} (\doxylink{classff_1_1ff__gatherer}{gt\+\_\+t}) that gathers the results computed by the {\itshape Workers}, which are ff\+\_\+nodes.

This class is defined in \doxylink{farm_8hpp}{farm.\+hpp}
