Add this component to a \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_skeleton_animation}{Skeleton\+Animation} or \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_skeleton_graphic}{Skeleton\+Graphic} Game\+Object to turn motion of a selected root bone into Transform or Rigid\+Body motion. Local bone translation movement is used as motion. All top-\/level bones of the skeleton are moved to compensate the root motion bone location, keeping the distance relationship between bones intact.

\doxysubsection{详细描述}
Add this component to a \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_skeleton_animation}{Skeleton\+Animation} or \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_skeleton_graphic}{Skeleton\+Graphic} Game\+Object to turn motion of a selected root bone into Transform or Rigid\+Body motion. Local bone translation movement is used as motion. All top-\/level bones of the skeleton are moved to compensate the root motion bone location, keeping the distance relationship between bones intact. 

Only compatible with \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_skeleton_animation}{Skeleton\+Animation} (or other components that implement \doxylink{interface_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_i_skeleton_component}{ISkeleton\+Component}, \doxylink{interface_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_i_skeleton_animation}{ISkeleton\+Animation} and \doxylink{interface_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_i_animation_state_component}{IAnimation\+State\+Component}). For {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_skeleton_mecanim}{Skeleton\+Mecanim}} please use \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_skeleton_mecanim_root_motion}{Skeleton\+Mecanim\+Root\+Motion} instead.
