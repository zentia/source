Applies this timeline to the skeleton. 


\begin{DoxyParams}{参数}
{\em skeleton} & The skeleton the timeline is being applied to. This provides access to the bones, slots, and other skeleton components the timeline may change.\\
\hline
{\em last\+Time} & The time this timeline was last applied. Timelines such as \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_event_timeline}{Event\+Timeline} trigger only at specific times rather than every frame. In that case, the timeline triggers everything between {\ttfamily last\+Time} (exclusive) and {\ttfamily time} (inclusive). Pass -\/1 the first time an animation is applied to ensure frame 0 is triggered.\\
\hline
{\em time} & The time in seconds that the skeleton is being posed for. Most timelines find the frame before and the frame after this time and interpolate between the frame values.\+If beyond the last frame, the last frame will be applied.\\
\hline
{\em events} & If any events are fired, they are added to this list. Can be null to ignore fired events or if the timeline does not fire events.\\
\hline
{\em alpha} & 0 applies the current or setup value (depending on {\ttfamily blend}). 1 applies the timeline value. Between 0 and 1 applies a value between the current or setup value and the timeline value.\+By adjusting {\ttfamily alpha} over time, an animation can be mixed in or out. {\ttfamily alpha} can also be useful to apply animations on top of each other (layering).\\
\hline
{\em blend} & Controls how mixing is applied when {\ttfamily alpha} \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<} 1.\\
\hline
{\em direction} & Indicates whether the timeline is mixing in or out. Used by timelines which perform instant transitions, such as \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_draw_order_timeline}{Draw\+Order\+Timeline} or \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_attachment_timeline}{Attachment\+Timeline}, and other such as \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_scale_timeline}{Scale\+Timeline}.\\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
