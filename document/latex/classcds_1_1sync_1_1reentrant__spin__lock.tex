Recursive spin lock. 

Allows recursive calls\+: the owner thread may recursive enter to critical section guarded by the spin-\/lock.

Template parameters\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\ttfamily Integral} one of integral atomic type\+: {\ttfamily unsigned int}, {\ttfamily int}, and others
\item {\ttfamily Backoff} backoff strategy. Used when spin lock is locked
