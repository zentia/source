You can clear this Texture cache by calling \doxylink{class_spine_1_1_unity_1_1_attachment_tools_1_1_atlas_utilities_a2abd8d25b488734bf7679e13b3dda982}{Atlas\+Utilities.\+Clear\+Cache()}. You may also want to call {\ttfamily Resources.\+Unload\+Unused\+Assets()} after that. Be aware that while this cleanup frees up memory, it is also a costly operation and will likely cause a spike in the framerate.
