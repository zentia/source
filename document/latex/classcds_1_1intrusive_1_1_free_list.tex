\doxysubsection{详细描述}
Lock-\/free free list 

Free list is a helper class intended for reusing objects instead of freeing them completely; this avoids the overhead of {\ttfamily \doxylink{mimalloc-override_8h_a99aa8fd9f4831e1b0fa9e1cdcde3e249}{malloc()}}, and also avoids its worst-\/case behavior of taking an operating system lock. So, the free list can be considered as a specialized allocator for objects of some type.

The algorithm is taken from \href{http://moodycamel.com/blog/2014/solving-the-aba-problem-for-lock-free-free-lists}{\texttt{ this article}}. The algo does not require any SMR like Hazard Pointer to prevent ABA problem.

There is \doxylink{classcds_1_1intrusive_1_1_tagged_free_list}{tagged pointers} variant of free list for processors with double-\/width CAS support.
